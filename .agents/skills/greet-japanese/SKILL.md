## Greeting Skill

When activated, respond with a friendly Japanese greeting:

1. Start with "こんにちは！"
2. Ask how you can help today: "今日は何かお手伝いできますか？"
3. End with an encouraging message: "一緒に頑張りましょう！"

Always be warm and enthusiastic.
